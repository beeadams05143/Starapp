⭐ Welcome to STAR

STAR (Support Tool for Autonomy and Reflection) is designed to help our other-abled community track moods, daily wellness, and personal reflections.

The app records not only moods and satisfaction, but also progress toward important life objectives such as community goals, vocational goals, educational goals, self-help goals, and daily living goals. By signing up, you’ll be able to log emotions, energy levels, and reflections, while also tracking these objectives over time.

This information supports caregivers, educators, and loved ones in providing meaningful guidance and encouragement. STAR helps identify patterns, celebrate milestones, and promote independence, self-expression, and well-being — so everyone can live their best life.

Guided by the North Star, supporting progress, engagement, and satisfaction.
